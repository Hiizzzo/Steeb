# Privacy Policy - STEEB Task Manager

**Last Updated:** October 13, 2025  
**Effective Date:** October 13, 2025  

## 1. Introduction

STEEB ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our task management application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Email Address**: Only when you create an account for authentication purposes
- **Name and Nickname**: Optional profile information you choose to provide
- **Task Data**: Tasks you create, including titles, descriptions, due dates, and completion status

### 2.2 Information Automatically Collected
- **Usage Analytics**: Local-only analytics about your productivity patterns (stored on your device only)
- **App Performance**: Technical data about app performance and crashes (stored locally)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Core Functionality
- **Authentication**: To sign you in and sync your tasks across devices
- **Task Management**: To store and organize your personal tasks
- **Productivity Insights**: To provide you with personal productivity analytics (calculated locally)

### 3.2 Data Storage
- **Local Storage**: All personal data is stored locally on your device using localStorage/IndexedDB
- **Cloud Sync**: Optional sync with Firebase Firestore only for your personal tasks when you create an account
- **No Third-Party Sharing**: We never share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing

## 4. Data We Do NOT Collect

- **Location Data**: We do not track your location
- **Advertising Identifiers**: We do not use IDFA or any advertising identifiers
- **Third-Party Analytics**: We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Analytics, or similar services
- **Cross-Device Tracking**: We do not track you across other apps or websites
- **Personal Information for Advertising**: We do not collect data for advertising purposes

## 5. Data Security

- **Encryption**: Data transmitted to Firebase is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
- **Local Storage**: Data on your device is protected by iOS security features
- **Access Control**: Only you have access to your personal task data

## 6. Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Stored on your device until you delete the app or clear the data
- **Cloud Data**: Stored in Firebase until you delete your account
- **Analytics**: Local analytics data can be deleted within the app settings

## 7. Your Rights

- **Access**: You can export your data at any time from the app settings
- **Deletion**: You can delete your account and all associated data
- **Correction**: You can update your profile information at any time
- **Opt-Out**: You can disable analytics tracking in app settings

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.

## 10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
- Email: privacy@steeb-app.com
- App: Through the settings in the STEEB app
